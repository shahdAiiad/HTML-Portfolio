Top C-drama Series of All Time According to Shahd

My top 3 C-drama Series of all-time.

The First Frost

I don't think any drama will beat this. I wish I could give this 1000/10. The First Frost portrays one of the most selfless and enduring love I have ever seen in any drama. It tells the story of Sang Yan and Yi Fan who fell in love in high school.

When I Fly Towards You

This is a feel good type of drama there are no love triangles, no unnecessary misunderstandings, just a beautifully written story about love and friendships.

Heddin Love

Hidden Love is a story about little girls and their little girl dreams of true love. I liked the realistic way the story about first love, which turns into everlasting love, was handled. This drama is so well written, engaging and well acted.
